---
term: "Pragmatic clinical trial"
definition: "A clinical trial designed to efficiently inform decision-making on the benefits, burdens, and risks of health interventions in representative populations by including pragmatic elements that (1) are partially or fully integrated into routine clinical practice and/or (2) streamline trial design and conduct. [Source 1](https://dx.doi.org/10.1177/1740774515598334)"
